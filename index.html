PLANTNET- Identify plant diseases using the power of Artificial Intelligence

Plantnet is a software application embodying algorithms that can identify the type of crop diseases through the image of the diseased crop. This will help farmers manage their crops effcectively and improve the yield economic returns. The technology uses machine learning approach to learn the signature of each disease from the many images of different manifestation of the same disease. Once identified PLANTNET uses a huge datatbase to come up with suggestions to mitigate further the development of the crop disease.
